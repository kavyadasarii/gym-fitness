Nutrition Is Half the Battle

You can train perfectly and still fall short of your goals if your nutrition isn't aligned with your training. FitCore's nutrition coaching service pairs expert guidance with practical, sustainable strategies that fit real life — not a temporary diet.

Your Personalised Nutrition Plan

Every plan starts with a full lifestyle and dietary assessment. Your coach analyses your current eating habits, training schedule, body composition goals, and food preferences before building a macro and calorie structure specific to you.

Custom Macros

Protein, carb, and fat targets tailored to your body weight, training load, and specific goal phase.

Meal Planning

Weekly meal plans with full recipes designed around foods you enjoy and can actually prepare.

Supplement Guide

Evidence-based supplement recommendations — only what's proven to make a meaningful difference.

Weekly Reviews

Every week your coach reviews your logs and adjusts targets based on progress and adherence.

Who Is This For?

Nutrition coaching is for anyone who trains consistently but isn't seeing the results they should be — or for people who simply don't know how to eat for their goals. Whether your focus is fat loss, muscle gain, or performance, the plan adjusts to match your phase.

"I'd been training for two years with barely any visible change. Eight weeks of proper nutrition coaching made more difference than the previous two years combined."
  1. Full dietary intake and lifestyle assessment
  2. Personalised macro targets and calorie structure
  3. Weekly meal plan with shopping list and recipes
  4. Daily food logging review by your nutrition coach
  5. Bi-weekly plan adjustments based on results
