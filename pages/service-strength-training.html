Progressive Strength, Built Right

FitCore's strength training program is built on the science of progressive overload — systematically increasing training stress over time to force continuous adaptation. Every program is periodised across 12-week blocks with planned deload weeks to maximise long-term gains and minimise injury risk.

Program Structure

Regardless of your experience level, every member goes through a structured assessment before beginning. This ensures the program matches your current strength, identifies mobility limitations, and establishes baseline numbers to track progress against.

Progressive Overload

Each week adds more volume or intensity. Your body is never allowed to plateau.

Compound Focus

Squat, deadlift, bench, and row form the backbone of every program. Isolation work supports the big lifts.

Periodisation

Training is planned in phases: hypertrophy, strength, and peak — each building on the last.

Injury Prevention

Mobility work, warm-up protocols, and deload weeks are built into every training block.

Who Is This For?

Whether you've never touched a barbell or you've been training for years and hit a wall, FitCore's strength program has a track for you. Beginner, intermediate, and advanced tracks ensure the training is always appropriately challenging.

"After 12 weeks I added 40kg to my deadlift. The programming was meticulous — every session had a purpose."
  1. Movement & strength baseline assessment
  2. Placement into beginner, intermediate, or advanced track
  3. 12-week periodised program with coach supervision
  4. Weekly technique check-ins on key lifts
  5. End-of-block testing and program re-design
