Train Smarter, Not Longer

HIIT at FitCore is structured, coach-led, and designed around the science of metabolic conditioning. Short bursts of maximum effort followed by controlled recovery periods push your cardiovascular system harder than any steady jog — in a fraction of the time.

The EPOC Advantage

One of the biggest benefits of HIIT is the afterburn effect — scientifically known as Excess Post-Exercise Oxygen Consumption (EPOC). After a HIIT session, your body continues burning calories at an elevated rate for up to 24 hours, making it one of the most efficient fat-loss tools available.

Timed Intervals

Heart Rate Zones

Coaches guide you through zones 3–5, ensuring you're working at the right intensity for your fitness level.

Group Energy

EPOC Effect

Who Is This For?

HIIT is ideal for people with limited time who want maximum results, those focused on fat loss while maintaining muscle, and anyone who finds traditional cardio boring. Sessions are scalable — coaches modify intensity for all fitness levels.
