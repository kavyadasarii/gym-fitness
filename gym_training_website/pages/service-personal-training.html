What Is Personal Training at FitCore?

Our personal training service goes far beyond a one-hour session. Every client receives a dedicated coach who designs, monitors, and adjusts their entire fitness journey — inside the gym and out. Through FitCore's client coaching dashboard, you stay connected to your program every single day.

The Coaching Dashboard

When you sign up for personal training, you get full access to your personal coaching dashboard — a private portal designed to keep you accountable between sessions.

Workout Plans

Access your current training program, view exercise demos, and log every session directly from the dashboard.

Meal Logging

Log your daily meals, track macros, and get feedback from your coach on your nutrition adherence.

Direct Messaging

Message your coach anytime with questions, updates, or for motivation. Response within 24 hours guaranteed.

Progress Tracking

Upload progress photos, log body measurements, and view your transformation timeline over weeks and months.

Who Is This For?

Personal training is ideal for anyone who wants results faster than they can achieve on their own — whether you're a complete beginner, returning after injury, or an experienced athlete wanting to break through a plateau. Your coach meets you exactly where you are.

"Having my coach available on the app changed everything. I wasn't just training 3 days a week anymore — I was being guided every single day."

What to Expect

  1. Initial consultation & fitness assessment (movement, strength, goals)
  2. Custom 4–8 week training program designed for your body
  3. Weekly in-person or virtual coaching sessions
  4. Dashboard access with daily check-in prompts
  5. Monthly program review and progression adjustment
